Ticket Costs

All persons traveling on the ferry require travel tickets. However, the cost of travel varies based on the age of the passenger
and is reflected in the table below:

  • An Infant ticket is free, but a ticket must be requested for an infant when purchasing the adult ticket.
  • Senior Citizens are Nationals of T&T 60 years & over with a valid T&T National ID or T&T Passport. This must be presented at the counter to validate ticket.
    • Day Time ScheduleDate DepartureTime Adults Teens Infants Elderly
    Adult (12 years & above) $75.00 $150.00 $150.00 $300.00
    Child (3 years to 11 years) $50.00 $100.00 $100.00 $200.00
    Infant (2 years and under) FREE FREE FREE FREE
    Senior Citizen (Nationals of T&T 60 years & over) $25.00 $50.00 $150.00 $300.00
