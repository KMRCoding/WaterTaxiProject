WELCOME!

Welcome to the Trinidad and Tobago Water Taxi Service's Online Ticking Website. Convenient Ticket Purchase at your service.

View Our Schedule!

You can take a look at our schedule that displays our planned voyages for an entire month. This way you can plan your trips and purchase tickets accordingly.

Purchase A Ticket!

We offer a simple and timely way to purchase a ticket online, as well as, payment options for credit, debit and visa cards. Our user friendly ticket purchase interface is designed to accomodate elderly and technologically inexperienced users.

View Our Vessels

You can take a look at our vessels, delving into information such as their capacity, service time and history. We cencourage users to brouse this page as it not only allows you to familiarize yourself with the vessels, but may allow you to make more informed decions in travel planning.

View Voyage Fares

There are many different factors that may influence the cost of a ticket. Familiarizing with them may enhace your experience.

Emergency Alert!

This functionality will allow you to report an emergency of any type to the our staff. It is meant to inform our staff of these emergencies even during a voyage.
